Chest and Lung Problems

Ischemic heart disease is the second biggest cause of death in the UK. There are nearly half a million hospital admissions associated with IHD each year. Evernear heart and Lung specialist Nurse can assess your symptoms and prevent possible hospital admissions.

.

End Stage Renal Disease

Dialysis starts when glomerular filtration rate (GFR) is 10-15% normal. Dialysis is needed lifelong unless a kidney transplant becomes available. Our experienced Clinical Renal Nurse specialist ambulatory peritoneal dialysis at the comfort of your home.

.

Mental Health Services

As many as one in four people will experience some form of mental illness at some point in their lives. Mental health problems range from reactions to normal life events, such as bereavement and relationship breakdowns and depression, to more complex conditions such as schizophrenia.
