About us

We are a group of Specialist Nurses that focus on excellent delivery of healthcare to the homes of our patients.

Our Mission

We are a healthcare company with a strong purpose of delivering quality healthcare to the community we serve. We are on a mission to eliminate the barrier of visting hospital by bringing the hospital to your community through our state-of-the-art medical van lead by competent specilist nurses.

Our vision is to be the unmached leader in improving quality and reducing the cost of healthcare for patients in the community we serve. We have a vision for preventive approach prevails because prevention is indeed better than cure.
